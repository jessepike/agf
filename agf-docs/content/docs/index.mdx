Welcome to the Agentic Governance Framework documentation.

## Evaluate AGF in 10 minutes

Three reading paths cover the framework end-to-end:

1. **[What is AGF?](/docs/overview/what-is-agf)** — the one-page orientation. Problem, architecture, and how AGF synthesizes existing standards rather than competing with them. (~3 min)
2. **[First 30 Days](/docs/overview/first-30-days)** — the week-by-week starter workplan for laying a Minimum Viable Control floor on a live agent. Concrete primitives, outputs, and exit criteria. (~4 min)
3. **[End-to-End Reference Walkthrough](/docs/overview/reference-walkthrough)** — one customer-refund decision threaded through all four Rings, with two complete Governance Decision Record YAML examples. (~3 min)

If those land, the deeper [Core Concepts in Order](/docs/overview/core-concepts) page is the right next step.
